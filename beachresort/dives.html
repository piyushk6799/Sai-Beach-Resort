Dive Site

  • Gale Reef

    The Gale Reef is celebrated for its unparalleled marine biodiversity. It is considered the epicenter of marine life diversity, with more than 1,500 species of fish and hundreds of coral species, many of which are found nowhere else on Earth.

    The reef system is famous for its stunning coral gardens. These coral formations are exceptionally healthy and vibrant, making Gale a top destination for divers and snorkelers from around the world. The coral reefs are teeming with colorful fish, invertebrates, and other marine creatures.

  • Dawson’s Reef

    The Dawson’s reef system is renowned for its rich marine biodiversity. It is home to a wide variety of coral species, fish, invertebrates, and other marine life. The vibrant coral formations and clear waters make it a popular destination for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts.

    The Dawson’s Reef primarily consists of hard corals, including species like elkhorn coral and staghorn coral. These corals provide essential habitat for numerous marine species and play a vital role in the health of the ecosystem.

  • Claire’s REEF

    The Claire’s reefs are known for their exceptional marine biodiversity, hosting a vast array of coral species, fish, and other marine life. The reefs are considered some of the most pristine in the world.

    The clear, warm waters around the islands make them a paradise for divers and snorkelers. Tourists flock to explore the colorful coral gardens and encounter diverse marine species. Conservation: To protect these vital ecosystems, marine conservation efforts are in place, including marine protected areas and strict regulations on fishing and tourism activities.
